Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We ask for at least days' notice. For events over 100 guests or during peak holiday weeks, a week or more is recommended.

Is there a minimum order?

Yes, our catering minimum is (before delivery fee).

Do you deliver?

We deliver anywhere within our service area for a flat fee, or you can pick up at our kitchen. Delivery vs. pickup is selected in the quote form.

Can you handle allergies and dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Note any allergies or restrictions in the special-requests field. We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options and label all trays.

How do I know how much to order?

Use the guest-count recommender in our quote builder — enter your headcount and we'll suggest an appropriate number of party trays as a starting point.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations 48+ hours before the event are fully refundable. Within 48 hours, a 50% fee applies as ingredients are ordered fresh.
